Redefining Family Dental Care in Kochi

At United Smiles Family Dentistry, we believe that a healthy smile is the foundation of overall well-being. Located conveniently opposite the High Court of Kerala, we offer a sanctuary where advanced dental science meets warm hospitality.

Whether you need routine family dental care, advanced clear aligners, or painless root canal treatments, our dedicated team of specialists—Dr. Annie, Dr. Basil, and Dr. Anu—are committed to providing personalized, affordable, and deeply compassionate care.

Our Dental Services

From preventive care to advanced orthodontics, we provide a full spectrum of dental treatments tailored to your unique needs.

Clear Aligners & Orthodontics

Advanced, invisible solutions to straighten your teeth discreetly. Achieve your perfect smile without traditional metal braces.

Root Canal Treatment

Painless, expertly performed root canals to save infected teeth and relieve severe toothaches immediately.

Family Dentistry

Gentle, comprehensive care for patients of all ages. We ensure a comfortable experience for children and adults alike.

Teeth Cleaning & Hygiene

Professional scaling and polishing to remove plaque, prevent cavities, and maintain optimal gum health.

Cosmetic Dentistry

Enhance the aesthetics of your smile with veneers, teeth whitening, and advanced restorative treatments.

Dental Consultation

Comprehensive oral examinations, x-rays, and personalized treatment planning by our expert specialists.
